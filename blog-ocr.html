How Does OCR Transform the Insurance Claim Process?

OCR is a technology that enables the conversion of scanned images, documents, or other sources into digital data. It helps transform the insurance claim process by automating data extraction from insurance claims documents, such as policyholder information, medical record, accident reports, and more. This automated process eliminates the need for manual data entry allowing faster and more accurate processing.

Insurers can reduce claims processing times, improve data accuracy, and reduce operational costs by streamlining the data extraction process. As per reports, insurers can save costs up to 60% by automating claim processing with OCR technology.
