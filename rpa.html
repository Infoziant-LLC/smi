What Is Process Mining and Why Does It Matter?

In process mining, data is used to visualize, analyze, and optimize business processes using event logs from existing IT systems. It’s like having a magnifying glass that reveals hidden inefficiencies, bottlenecks, and deviations from your ideal workflows. In order to improve processes, businesses can gain a better understanding of how they are actually executed in real-time.

A Precision Approach to RPA: Automating Repetitive Tasks

* Robotic Process Automation (RPA), businesses can automate repetitive, rule-based tasks. Using RPA bots, humans can perform tasks like data entry, transaction processing, and communication between systems without intervening. In addition to saving time, this improves accuracy and frees up employees to work on higher-value tasks.

* In spite of the fact that RPA can automate tasks, it is still dependent on predefined processes. This is where process mining steps in to fill in the gaps, making RPA smarter and more efficient.
