EDI for Healthcare

For the healthcare industry, SMI implemented distinct modules tailored to various scenarios, ensuring compatibility with existing HSA systems and additional business needs, such as lines of credit. We integrated HSA claims through a horizontal integration design, allowing calculations, transformations, and seamless transmission to online applications. All transformations and mapping rules were designed to maintain EDI compliance.

EDI for Manufacturing

In the manufacturing sector, SMI utilized the Smooks data integration tool to efficiently convert EDI files into comma-separated (CSV) formats. Through the use of Java scripts, large volumes of CSV files were loaded into the database in seconds. The system can import very large CSV files in under 60 seconds, delivering significant time savings to the client.

EDI for the Automobile Industry

SMI streamlined the automobile industry’s processes through kitting, a method that consolidates various sub/vendor-based systems. After thorough analysis of vendor business processes, we implemented a solution that reduced operational costs. The batching concept played a crucial role in ensuring the success of this project.
