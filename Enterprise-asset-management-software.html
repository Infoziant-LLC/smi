Manufacturing Industry

EAM plays a vital role in process management across manufacturing sectors, including defense, electronics, industrial products, and automotive. It enables thorough tracking of the product lifecycle and efficient management of operational requirements, ensuring streamlined processes and enhanced productivity.

Energy & Utilities

Energy and utility companies depend on critical assets like power lines and pipelines to deliver services, making it essential to integrate scheduling and geospatial data into their infrastructure for efficient management and reliable service delivery.

Transportation & Logistics

EAM solutions are invaluable for logistics and service operations, providing complete visibility into inventory and assets. Key features include fuel management, spare parts tracking, bay scheduling, and driver logs, ensuring efficient and well-organized operations across the board.

Construction & Engineering

Construction and engineering firms can greatly benefit from asset management solutions designed for the digital asset lifecycle, incorporating advanced technologies such as drones and digital twins to meet their evolving needs.

Oil & Gas

Our software gathers data from sensors and IoT devices on oil rigs, pipelines, and refineries, offering real-time insights into asset performance, equipment health, and environmental conditions. These insights enable early detection of potential failures, optimize maintenance schedules, and help prevent costly downtime.

Healthcare Industry

EAM software supports the healthcare sector by managing the critical interdependencies between equipment readiness and facility operations. It enables precise asset location tracking, ensures regulatory compliance in reporting, integrates smoothly with health information systems, and provides continuous monitoring of facility conditions for optimal performance.

Retail Industry

Retail operations rely on a range of essential equipment to ensure smooth store functionality, including storage areas, displays, building infrastructure, and point-of-sale systems. As digital commerce grows, new processes like pick-up services and customer lockers require additional assets to adapt to evolving customer demands.

Facilities & Property Management

EAM is essential for offices, educational institutions, and public sector facilities, enabling effective monitoring and management of building systems, furnishings, operational equipment, and grounds. With evolving work practices, facility management has gained increased importance, and comprehensive reporting is now vital in shaping long-term strategies for building assets.

Government & Public Sector

Our software enables government agencies to maintain a detailed inventory of public assets, including buildings, roads, bridges, parks, and other facilities. It tracks asset conditions, schedules maintenance, and logs repair history, ensuring efficient management and upkeep.

Chemicals, Petroleum, and Mining

In the chemicals, petroleum, and mining industries, safety, reliability, compliance, and performance integration are essential. EAM systems are vital for streamlining maintenance practices, fostering collaboration, and reducing costs through standardization, ensuring operational efficiency and regulatory adherence.

Aerospace and Defense

Aerospace and defense organizations rely on EAM software to maintain the continuous availability and readiness of critical assets across their entire lifecycle.

Nuclear Power

In the nuclear industry, organizations emphasize robust work and asset management to meet strict regulatory standards. Detailed state management, workflows, escalations, and e-signatures ensure compliance with health, safety, and security regulations, addressing the industry's highest standards for operational integrity.
