Work Order Management

Work order management involves the process of creating, tracking, and completing maintenance and repair tasks within a facility. Innomaint can help facilities to prioritize work orders based on urgency and allocate resources to the tasks. It also provides real-time updates on the work order overdue/exceeds the scheduled time, the status of work orders, enabling facility managers to track progress and make informed decisions.
Eliminate paperwork
Seamless maintenance process
Improve operational efficiency

AMC Management

AMC management helps track and manage contracts, schedule maintenance tasks, and provide real-time updates on the status of assets. It can also help manage service requests and prioritize tasks based on their criticality and urgency. Facility managers can easily create invoices and keep track of payments by service providers and ensure that all maintenance tasks are completed on time, reducing the likelihood of equipment breakdowns and downtime.
Ensures compliance with regulations & standards
Better data management
Reduces the risk of accidents and injuries

Asset Management

Asset management refers to the efficient and effective tracking and maintenance of assets such as equipment, machinery, and physical facilities within an organization. Innomaint helps facility managers to monitor the assets’ lifecycle, maintain accurate inventory records, and schedule maintenance and repairs to ensure their proper functioning.
Save time and money
Valuable asset utilization
Spare parts forecasting

Spare Parts Management

Innomaint keeps track of the availability of spare parts, purchase orders, stock levels, vendor performance and their location in real-time. It also alerts the maintenance team when stock levels are low and generates purchase orders automatically, ensuring that the spare parts are available when needed.

With Innomaint now anyone can raise a request through one common QR code within facilities.

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
Manage various inventories across your business locations from your mobile.

Associate QR codes to each inventory item to reduce inventory management costs by storing & retrieving all the information associated with a simple mobile scan.

  • Have accurate information on closing stock after each transaction
  • Maintain the right balance between overstocking and shortage
  • Acquire the right stock at right time with minimum reorder alerts
FIXED ASSET AUDITING
Ensure the primary assets are at their current location along with identity verification.

Verify serial number, make, model, manufacturer, good operating condition, and placement of assets using QR codes, RFID, and assess their current value.

  • Assess current value using several prevailing depreciation methods
  • Ensure the safety of assets and verify they are put to desired use
  • Automate physical audits to a greater extent using digital tools
ASSET LIFE CYCLE MANAGEMENT
End-to-End Asset Life Cycle Management from acquisition to disposal.

The process ensures an organization maintains the profitability of its assets at their maximum for the entire life cycle of the asset.

  • Prolongs asset longevity
  • Keeps a check on maintenance costs
  • Enables adherence to budgets
OEE MONITORING
Measure productivity levels of assets live w.r.t Availability, Performance & Quality.

A world-class IoT technique that helps you evaluate asset productivity by automatically calculating metrics based on provided inputs.

  • Exclusive dashboard for OEE calculation
  • Helps uncover reasons for underperformance
  • Establishes quantifiable performance benchmarks
ASSET TRACKING WITH BLE/RFID/QR CODE
Trace, Track & Manage moving assets in real-time with BLE Beacon.

Mount BLE Beacons/RFID sensors on assets to enable live tracking. These sensors report entry and exit events within the reader's range, providing robust tracking capabilities.

  • Live moving asset tracking via mobile
  • 2x data transfer and communication speeds
  • Robust and precise indoor tracking
INTERACTIVE ASSET FLOOR PLAN
A pictorial representation of asset hierarchy with dynamic schedule triggering.

Simplify complex floor layouts with zoom in and zoom out capabilities, providing clear instructions for in-house and contracted technicians.

  • Enable technicians to quickly find assets in a complex set-up
  • Trigger schedules for multiple assets from a single site map
  • Ensure many assets do not simultaneously pull out from production cycles
