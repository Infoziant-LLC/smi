Freight Forwarding Automation How It Works

Multi-Channel Enquiry Handling

1.Channels: Customers can submit enquiries through multiple platforms, including:

  • Enquiry Forms
  • Chatbot
  • WhatsApp
  • Emails

2.The system consolidates all incoming enquiries from these channels into one dashboard for easy management.

Enquiry Bot for Classification & Assignment

1.Enquiry Bot: An AI-powered enquiry bot scans each enquiry, classifies it based on content (e.g., pricing, shipment status, booking), and routes it to the correct department (sales, customer support, or operations).

2.Mobile Conversations: The enquiry bot also handles conversations over mobile and messaging platforms (e.g., WhatsApp), providing a seamless customer experience.

Email Bot for Automated Processing

The email bot plays a critical role in automating your email workflows for freight forwarding.

1.Email Classification: The email bot scans all incoming emails, classifying them based on their content and extracting relevant details (such as booking numbers or shipment details).

2.Order Fulfillment Posting: Automatically processes email attachments, such as booking confirmations or purchase orders, and posts them directly into the system for order fulfillment.

3.Assignment to Teams: The system automatically assigns each email to the appropriate team — whether it’s sales, customer support, or operations — for further action.

4.Status Updates: Once processed, the bot automatically updates the booking status in the system, keeping the workflow transparent and ensuring smooth collaboration across departments.

AI Co-Pilot for Decision Support

AI Co-Pilot: Provides team members with intelligent suggestions for actions such as pricing adjustments, order approvals, or customer communication updates, making decision-making faster and more accurate.

Automated Booking and Fulfillment

1.Workflow Automation: Once the enquiry or booking is processed, the system updates the relevant booking statuses, tracks progress, and notifies the customer.

2.Human-in-the-Loop: The AI Co-Pilot keeps staff in the loop while automating repetitive tasks, ensuring human oversight and improving accuracy.
