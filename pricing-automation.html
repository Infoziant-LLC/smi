How Enquiry Automation Works in Freight Forwarding

Data Collection through Multiple Channels

Data from shipment details (weight, volume, destination) is collected via multiple channels:

  • Enquiry Forms
  • Emails
  • CRM Systems
  • Integrated Shipping Platforms

Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) automatically extracts data from incoming documents like invoices and shipment requests.

AI-Powered Calculation and Rule Application

AI Bots analyze all the collected data and apply rules based on:

  • Shipment type and destination
  • Carrier rates and surcharges
  • Volume or weight-based calculations
  • Customs and handling fees (if applicable)

Real-time market data is also factored in to ensure quotes reflect current industry rates.

AI Co-Pilot-Assisted Decision Making

The AI Co-Pilot reviews the generated quote, offering suggestions such as:

  • More cost-effective carriers or routes
  • Potential discounts for frequent customers
  • Recommendations for upselling or cross-selling additional services
  • Flagging discrepancies or errors for human review before finalization

Automated Quote Generation and Communication

The final quote is auto-generated, including all relevant fees (base rate, surcharges, customs, etc.).

The quote is delivered via email or displayed on the customer portal, and an automated follow-up email is scheduled to ensure the quote is reviewed promptly.

Quote Status Tracking and Reporting

The system tracks customer interactions with the quote (viewed, accepted, declined).

Analytics provide insights into quote success rates, allowing for pricing strategy improvements and adjustments.
