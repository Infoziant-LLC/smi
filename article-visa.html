Important updates and changes to Dubai visa regulation 2023

The UAE government implemented the upgraded visa system in October 2022, and since then, the visa regulations have undergone a number of modifications. Let us see:

  • ICP Fee Increase ICP raises service fees by AED 100 for all services, including Emirates IDs, visas, and residency permits.
  • Visa Stamping UAE replaces passport visa stamps with Emirates ID, streamlining the residency process.
  • Visit Visa Extension UAE no longer allows visit visa extensions without exiting; travelers must leave, apply, and re-enter with the new visa, often using quick trips to nearby countries like Oman.
  • Dubai Exit Permit The exit permit is only required when leaving via Dubai; other Emirates do not need it and can be obtained at the airport or land border immigration desks.

4 Possibilities to living in the UAE

There are many ways to live in the UAE with different types of visas available. Whether starting a business or seeking employment, here are your options:

Company sponsored visa:

Secure an offer letter from a UAE organization to live and work there. After signing the offer letter, the company will initiate the visa process, which includes a medical fitness test, fingerprinting for biometric data, and obtaining an Emirates ID.

Note: The employer is responsible for all recruitment and visa costs as per UAE labor laws.

Sponsor family visa:

If you work, own a business, or hold property in the UAE, you can sponsor residency visas for your family, spouse, or children.

Golden visa

For a stable, long-term residence, eligible individuals can apply for the Golden Visa, allowing a 10-year stay with sponsorship for family members and support staff.

Eligible categories:
  • Exceptional talents: Art, culture, innovation, sports, digital tech.
  • Professionals: Scientists, executives, engineers, health, education, IT, business, legal professionals.
  • Outstanding students: Top graduates from local and global universities.
  • Humanitarian pioneers: Honored members of groups, notable volunteers, donors.
  • Frontline workers: COVID-19 response personnel.

Golden visa holders enjoy benefits like permanent residency, discounts, and more.

Resident visa

A UAE Residence Visa is essential for foreigners looking to live and work in the UAE long-term, offering benefits like opening bank accounts, securing loans, visa-free travel, enrolling children in schools, and accessing healthcare.

Types of residence visas:
  • Work visa: For foreigners employed in the UAE.
  • Student visa: For international students enrolling in UAE institutions.
  • Investment visa: For foreign investors and entrepreneurs.
  • Retirement visa: For elderly foreigners meeting financial requirements.
  • Green visa: Self-sponsoring visa with a 5-year validity, allowing sponsorship of immediate relatives and a 6-month grace period after expiration.
