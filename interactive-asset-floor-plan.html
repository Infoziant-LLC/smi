Streamline Asset Management

Maintenance managers can quickly locate the assets, in a complex asset set-up via the web app. Upon spotting the asset, managers can help technicians get to work orders quickly. By this way, line managers can ensure the majority of the assets do not simultaneously shut themselves from production cycles.

The map makes it easy for managers to access outstanding and overdue work orders on assets and current uptime/downtime details on each asset.

Trigger schedules readily from a single site map with a centralized scheduler in-built with a 52-week PPM calendar. Faster identification of an asset also paves the way for quicker work order resolution. A well-organized facility has an interactive site map for every floor accessible via a centralized digital hub.

Zoom in and zoom out on plans and maps for the most convenient identification.
