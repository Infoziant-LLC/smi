Our Features

Facility Management
Software Features

Work Order Management

Work order management simplifies task tracking and completion. Innomaint aids prioritization, resource allocation, and real-time updates on delays and progress.

AMC Management

AMC management tracks contracts, schedules maintenance, and provides real-time asset updates. It manages service requests, prioritizes tasks, and streamlines invoicing and payments, reducing breakdowns and downtime.

Asset Management

Asset management ensures efficient tracking and maintenance of equipment and facilities. Innomaint aids in monitoring asset lifecycles, maintaining inventory, and scheduling upkeep for optimal performance.

Spare Parts Management

Innomaint tracks spare parts, purchase orders, stock levels, and vendor performance in real-time. It alerts teams on low stock and auto-generates purchase orders, ensuring timely availability.

QR Code Scanning

Technicians can scan QR codes via smartphones to access maintenance history, warranties, and manuals. Innomaint enables request submissions through a single QR code within facilities.

Geo Location and GPS Tracking

Track technicians' routes and distances via GPS for faster service and real-time asset location. Innomaint lets field employees log attendance by punching in and out seamlessly.

Dashboard and Reporting

Innomaint's dashboard offers customizable MMR reports on maintenance, work orders, and asset utilization. Insights help identify trends and drive efficient, cost-effective decisions.

Webex

Webex Teams is a cloud-based collaboration platform that supports video meetings, messaging, file sharing, and whiteboarding. It offers a persistent virtual meeting space where both in-office and remote teams can collaborate seamlessly. Technologies Used: ReactJS, Ruby on Rails

ERP Application

Our ERP application streamlines and tracks business transactions, enabling multi-level entity engagement, smart stock ordering and management, controlled fabrication of finished goods, and mapping of marketing activities. It also provides dynamic dashboards, comprehensive reporting, and security management. Technology Used: .Net, MVC, SQL
