Building Management System

Our Building Management System (BMS) offers centralized, automatic control for HVAC, lighting, and other essential building systems. This IoT solution for building management ensures secure data transmission, control, and storage, making it ideal for optimizing operational efficiency. One of the key challenges addressed by our solution is the integration of multiple communication protocols, including BACnet, MODBUS, and MBUS, ensuring seamless communication across all building systems.

Smart Energy Solutions

Our Smart Energy Solutions provide a robust smart metering system that tackles the challenges faced by businesses and households without proper metering infrastructure. This IoT-enabled smart metering solution generates detailed reports and offers data visualization to give insightful analytics on transactions, operations, and energy consumption across meters, devices, and buildings. With this solution, businesses can make data-driven decisions to improve energy efficiency and reduce costs.

Welding Quality Monitoring System

The Welding Quality Monitoring System is designed to monitor power consumption in welding machines using advanced IoT sensors. By capturing and analysing data on voltage and current, our solution accurately determines the quality of the welding process. This IoT-based welding monitoring system helps manufacturers improve quality control, reduce waste, and ensure compliance with industry standards.
